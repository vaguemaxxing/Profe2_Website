Process Flow Description

  1. 1.1 Take Order: Waiter receives customer order and records it
  2. 1.2 Submit to Kitchen: Order slip is created and sent to kitchen (Process 2.0)
  3. 1.3 Serve Food: Prepared food is collected from kitchen and served to customer
  4. 1.4 Calculate Bill: Final bill is calculated based on order details
  5. 1.5 Process Payment: Payment is collected, receipt issued, and transaction recorded
